Calories Counter

The calorie calculator allows you to determine the amount of energy that you need to receive from the body daily, depending on your height, weight, gain and degree of physical activity (caloric norm).

Also, this version of the calorie calculator, as a result of the calculation, gives recommendations on the value of calorie indicators, blood vessels for weight loss. Calorie schedule size recommendations are in the form of a rough schedule by day of the week and are useful for those who want to lose weight through diet.
